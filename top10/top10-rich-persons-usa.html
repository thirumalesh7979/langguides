Top 10 richest persons of USA

The United States is home to some of the wealthiest individuals in the world, with fortunes made in various industries

Here are the top 10 richest people in the USA as of 2023 according to Forbes' Real-Time Billionaires List, and the things they did to achieve their wealth.

USA Richest People List:

  1. Bernard Arnault and family
  2. Elon Musk
  3. Jeff Bezos
  4. Larry Ellison
  5. Bill Gates
  6. Warren Buffett
  7. Larry Page
  8. Carlos Slim Helu and family
  9. Steve Ballmer
  10. Sergey Brin

1. Bernard Arnault and family

With a net worth of 236.6 billion, Bernard Arnault is the CEO of LVMH, which is the world's largest luxury goods company. Arnault has made his fortune through his company's brands, such as Louis Vuitton, Dior, and Sephora.

2. Elon Musk

The CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, Elon Musk has a net worth of $176.9 billion. Musk's wealth comes from his ventures in electric cars, space exploration, and renewable energy.

3. Jeff Bezos

The founder and former CEO of Amazon, Jeff Bezos has a net worth of $133.3 billion. Bezos' wealth primarily comes from Amazon, which he started as an online bookstore in 1994 and grew into a global e-commerce giant.

4. Larry Ellison

With a net worth of $121.8 billion, Larry Ellison is the co-founder and former CEO of Oracle Corporation, which is one of the largest providers of enterprise software solutions in the world. Ellison has made his fortune through his company's software and hardware products, as well as strategic investments in companies like Tesla.

5. Bill Gates

The co-founder of Microsoft, Bill Gates has a net worth of $114 billion. Gates made his fortune through Microsoft, which he co-founded in 1975 and became one of the world's most valuable companies.

6. Warren Buffett

The CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, Warren Buffett has a net worth of $112.7 billion. Buffett made his fortune through investments and is known as the "Oracle of Omaha" for his investment strategies.

7. Larry Page

The co-founder of Google, Larry Page has a net worth of $100.1 billion. Page made his fortune through Google, which he started with Sergey Brin in 1998 and grew into the world's most popular search engine.

8. Carlos Slim Helu and family

With a net worth of $99.1 billion, Carlos Slim Helu is a Mexican businessman and philanthropist who made his fortune through his telecommunications company, America Movil. Slim also has investments in other industries, such as banking, mining, and real estate.

9. Steve Ballmer

The former CEO of Microsoft, Steve Ballmer has a net worth of $96 billion. Ballmer made his fortune through his tenure at Microsoft, where he helped the company become a dominant player in the tech industry.

10. Sergey Brin

The co-founder of Google, Sergey Brin has a net worth of $95.8 billion. Brin made his fortune through Google, which he started with Larry Page in 1998 and grew into one of the world's most valuable companies.

Conclusion:

These individuals have achieved success through their business acumen, strategic investments, and ability to innovate and adapt in their respective industries. Their wealth not only reflects their own success but also their impact on the global economy.
