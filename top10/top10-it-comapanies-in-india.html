Top 10 IT Companies in India

India is home to a vibrant IT industry, with many companies offering software development, IT consulting, and outsourcing services. Here are the top 10 IT companies in India and the average salary for a software employee according to Glassdoor.

List of Top 10 Indian IT Companies:

  1. Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)
  2. Infosys
  3. Wipro
  4. HCL Technologies
  5. Tech Mahindra
  6. IBM India
  7. Accenture India
  8. Capgemini India
  9. Cognizant Technology Solutions
  10. Mindtree

1. Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

TCS is the largest IT company in India and employs over 469,000 people worldwide. The average salary for a software engineer at TCS is around ₹6,62,493 per year.

2. Infosys

Infosys is a multinational corporation that offers IT consulting, software development, and outsourcing services. The annual average salary for a software engineer at Infosys is approximately ₹5,44,875.

3. Wipro

Wipro is a multinational corporation that provides IT consulting, software development, and outsourcing services. The average salary for a software engineer at Wipro is around ₹5,35,342 per year.

4. HCL Technologies

HCL Technologies is a multinational corporation that provides IT consulting, software development, and outsourcing services. The average salary for a software engineer at HCL Technologies is around ₹6,62,238 per year.

5. Tech Mahindra

Tech Mahindra is a multinational corporation that provides IT consulting, software development, and outsourcing services. The average salary for a software engineer at Tech Mahindra is around ₹6,08,244 per year.

6. IBM India

IBM India is the Indian subsidiary of IBM and provides software development, IT consulting, and outsourcing services. The average salary for a software engineer at IBM India is around ₹9,20,797 per year.

7. Accenture India

Accenture India is the Indian subsidiary of Accenture and provides IT consulting and outsourcing services. The average salary for a software engineer at Accenture India is around ₹6,65,192 per year.

8. Capgemini India

Capgemini India is the Indian subsidiary of Capgemini and provides IT consulting, software development, and outsourcing services. The average salary for a software engineer at Capgemini India is around ₹5,50,442 per year.

9. Cognizant Technology Solutions

Cognizant Technology Solutions is a multinational corporation that specializes in IT consulting, software development, and outsourcing services. The average annual salary for a software engineer at Cognizant Technology Solutions is approximately ₹8,31,404.

10. Mindtree

Mindtree is a multinational corporation that provides IT consulting and software development services. The average salary for a software engineer at Mindtree is around ₹7,43,182 per year.

Conclusion:

The IT industry in India is growing rapidly, with many companies offering highly competitive salaries for software engineers. The average salary for a software engineer in these companies varies based on location, experience, and job title. Glassdoor is a reliable source of information for job seekers looking for salary information in the IT industry in India.
