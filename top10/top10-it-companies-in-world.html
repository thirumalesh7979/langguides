Top 10 IT Companies in the World

Over the past decade, the IT industry has experienced exponential growth due to the rising global demand for technology, making it one of the fastest-growing sectors worldwide.

Here are the top 10 IT companies in the world and the average salary for a software employee according to Glassdoor.

Top 10 IT Companies List:

  1. Microsoft
  2. Amazon
  3. Alphabet
  4. Apple
  5. IBM
  6. Facebook
  7. Intel
  8. Cisco
  9. Oracle
  10. Dell

1. Microsoft

Founded in 1975, Microsoft has become one of the largest IT companies in the world, with a market cap of over $2 trillion. The average salary for a software engineer at Microsoft is around $121,622 per year.

2. Amazon

Founded in 1994, Amazon started as an online bookstore and has now become the largest online retailer in the world. The average salary for a software engineer at Amazon is around $129,868 per year.

3. Alphabet

Founded in 2015, Alphabet serves as the parent company of Google. The company has a market cap of over $1.4 trillion. The average salary for a software engineer at Alphabet is around $144,995 per year.

4. Apple

Founded in 1976, Apple is known for its innovative products such as the iPhone, iPad, and MacBook. The average salary for a software engineer at Apple is around $149,010 per year.

5. IBM

Founded in 1911, IBM is one of the oldest IT companies in the world. The company has a market cap of over $100 billion. The average salary for a software engineer at IBM is around $95,437 per year.

6. Facebook

Founded in 2004, Facebook is the largest social networking platform in the world, with over 2 billion users. The average salary for a software engineer at Facebook is around $151,152 per year.

7. Intel

Founded in 1968, Intel is a multinational semiconductor chip maker. The company's market capitalization exceeds $200 billion. The average salary for a software engineer at Intel is around $104,527 per year.

8. Cisco

Founded in 1984, Cisco is a multinational technology conglomerate that specializes in networking hardware. The average salary for a software engineer at Cisco is around $120,387 per year.

9. Oracle

Founded in 1977, Oracle is a multinational computer technology corporation that specializes in developing and marketing software. The average salary for a software engineer at Oracle is around $119,137 per year.

10. Dell

Founded in 1984, Dell is a multinational computer technology corporation that specializes in developing and selling computer hardware. The average salary for a software engineer at Dell is around $87,335 per year.

Conclusion:

The IT industry is a highly lucrative field, with some of the top IT companies in the world offering highly competitive salaries for software engineers. The average salary for a software engineer at these companies varies based on location, experience, and job title.
