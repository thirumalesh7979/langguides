Top 10 Richest Football Players in our Planet

Soccer, or football as it's known in many parts of the world, is not just a passion for millions of fans, it's also a lucrative career for some of the world's best players. Here are the top 10 richest soccer players in the world according to Forbes as of 2021.

  1. Lionel Messi
  2. Cristiano Ronaldo
  3. Neymar Jr.
  4. Kylian Mbappé
  5. Mohamed Salah
  6. Paul Pogba
  7. Andres Iniesta
  8. Gareth Bale
  9. Robert Lewandowski
  10. Mesut Özil

1. Lionel Messi

With a net worth of $130 million, Messi is not just one of the greatest soccer players of all time, but also the richest. He plays for Paris Saint-Germain after spending two decades with Barcelona.

2. Cristiano Ronaldo

Ronaldo's net worth is estimated at $120 million, and he has played for some of the biggest soccer clubs in the world, including Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. He currently plays for Manchester United.

3. Neymar Jr.

The Brazilian forward's net worth is $95 million. Neymar Jr. currently plays for Paris Saint-Germain.

4. Kylian Mbappé

his French forward has a net worth of $43 million and is considered one of the brightest young talents in soccer. He currently plays for Paris Saint-Germain alongside Messi and Neymar Jr.>

5. Mohamed Salah

The Egyptian striker's net worth is estimated at $38 million. He is a member of the Liverpool football club in the English Premier League.

6. Paul Pogba

This French midfielder has a net worth of $34 million and currently plays for Manchester United.

7. Andres Iniesta

This Spanish midfielder has a net worth of $32.5 million. Iniesta currently plays for Vissel Kobe in the Japanese league.

8. Gareth Bale

The Welsh forward's net worth is $30 million. He currently plays for Real Madrid.

9. Robert Lewandowski

The Polish striker has a net worth of $28 million and is one of the most prolific goal-scorers in the world. He currently plays for Bayern Munich.

10. Mesut Özil

This German midfielder has a net worth of $24 million. Özil currently plays for Fenerbahçe in Turkey.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, these soccer players not only possess incredible skills on the field but also have made a fortune from their careers. From Messi and Ronaldo to the rising stars like Mbappé and Salah, these players have solidified their places in soccer history and continue to amaze fans with their performances on the field.
