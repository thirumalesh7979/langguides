1. Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella is the CEO of Microsoft, one of the largest software companies in the world. Net Worth: $300 million.

He holds a degree in electrical engineering from Manipal Institute of Technology in India and a master's degree in computer science from the University of Wisconsin. He worked at Sun Microsystems before joining Microsoft in 1992.

2. Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet, the parent company of Google. His net worth is estimated to be around $600 million. He holds a degree in metallurgical engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology in Kharagpur and a master's degree in materials science and engineering from Stanford University. He joined Google in 2004 and became CEO in 2015.

3. Tim Cook

Tim Cook is the CEO of Apple, one of the largest tech companies in the world. He studied industrial engineering at Auburn University and worked at IBM before joining Apple in 1998. He became CEO in 2011, following the death of Steve Jobs. Net Worth: $1.4 billion

5. Ginni Rometty

6. Marillyn Hewson

Marillyn Hewson is the former CEO of Lockheed Martin, a multinational aerospace and defense company. Her net worth is estimated to be around $20 million. 7. Brian Cornell

Brian Cornell is the CEO of Target, one of the largest retail companies in the world. His net worth is estimated to be around $60 million. He holds a degree in mechanical engineering from the University of California, Los Angeles, and an MBA from the Anderson School of Management at the University of California, Los Angeles. He started his career at PepsiCo before moving into the retail industry.

8. James Quincey

James Quincey is the CEO of Coca-Cola, one of the largest beverage companies in the world. He holds a degree in electronic engineering from the University of Liverpool and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. He started his career at Coca-Cola in 1996 and became CEO in 2017. Net Worth: $18 million.

9. Doug McMillon

Doug McMillon is the CEO of Walmart, one of the largest retail companies in the world. His net worth is estimated to be around $100 million. He holds a degree in business administration from the University of Arkansas. He started his career at Walmart as a summer associate in 1984 and became CEO in 2014.

10. Jim Hagemann Snabe

Jim Hagemann Snabe is the former CEO of SAP, one of the largest enterprise software companies in the world. He holds a degree in business administration from the University of Copenhagen. He started his career at SAP in 1990 and became co-CEO in 2010, before becoming sole CEO in 2014. Net Worth: $50 million
